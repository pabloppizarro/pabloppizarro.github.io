FollowJC GO | Mobile Location-base.

Follow JC Go! is a location-based, multiplayer, 3D, and free-to-play game. It enables players to create their own eTeam (Evangelization Team), comprising of friends, biblical characters, saints, blesseds, and Marian advocations. The eTeam assists players in building a collection of characters who will accompany them in their daily lives and on their pilgrimage to WYD 2023 in Portugal, as well as other religious events worldwide. To achieve this, players must complete missions, search for and answer trivia questions to acquire collectibles for their eTeam, and stay connected with friends through a messaging service.
