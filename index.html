FollowJC GO | Mobile Location-base.

Follow JC Go! is a location-based, multiplayer, 3D, and free-to-play game. It allows players to form their eTeam (Evangelization Team), consisting of friends, biblical characters, saints, blesseds, and Marian advocations. The eTeam will help players build a collection of characters who will accompany them in their daily life and pilgrimage to WYD 2023 in Portugal and other religious events around the world. To do this, they must complete missions, search for and answer trivia questions to add collectibles to their eTeam, and stay in touch with friends through a messaging service. Some links to check their on Play Store and App Store. About the game in the News: IGN Spain, Infobae, BBC and The Verge.
